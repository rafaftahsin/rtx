Types of printers 

- Inkjet 
  - The ink is coggled if it's not used for few weeks.
- Laser Printer
  - No problem with ink coggling 
  - Price is a bit higher
- Thermal printer 
  - Uses Different types of paper to print
  - Fast
  - Doesn't require ink to print
  - various ATM & POS Machines uses it.
